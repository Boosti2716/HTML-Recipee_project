Best pizza ever

Pizza: a circle of dough topped with cheese, sauce, and other ingredients, baked until golden brown and bubbling. A delicious and versatile food that can be enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients:

  • 1Kg flour
  • 1 tbspoon fine salt
  • 2 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 big spoons olive oil
  • 30g fresh yeast

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough. You can use store-bought dough or make your own. If making your own, simply combine flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil in a bowl and knead until smooth. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.

  2. Prepare the sauce. Heat some tomato sauce in a saucepan over medium heat. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste.

  3. Roll out the dough. Once the dough has risen, preheat your oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit. Lightly flour a work surface and roll out the dough into a 12-inch circle.

  4. Add the toppings. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough, then top with your desired toppings. Some popular toppings include mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, and peppers.

  5. Bake the pizza. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Enjoy! Cut the pizza into slices and serve immediately.
